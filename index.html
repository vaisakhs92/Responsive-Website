about them



A South American mammal of the camel family, Lama glama, used as a domestic beast of burden and a source of wool and meat. These animals who will rule the world one day. If you do not worship these animals they will feed you to their babies during the End of the World. But otherwise they are known to be the most awesome animals in the world.

Fun facts

Size

Llamas are large animals. They can be 6 feet tall (at the head) and reach between 280 and 450 pounds of weight.

More llamas

Llamas do not have specific time of mating. Male usually chases female for up to 10 minutes until she is finally ready to mate.

Vegeterian

Llamas are herbivores (eat only plants). They eat grass and ferns mainly. Llama on the farm may eat 6 pounds of hay per day.

Smart

Llamas are very intelligent. When the load on its back is too heavy, llama will refuse to carry it until some of the load is removed.

Longevity

Lifespan of llamas is between 20 and 30 years. Llamas are very social animals. They live in groups composed of up to 20 animals.

Badass

Llamas have inherited fear of coyote, mountain lions and other dog-like animals. Some llamas are "guards" of the sheep.
